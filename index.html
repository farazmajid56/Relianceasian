Who We Are

Reliance Asian Company LLC is a trading firm that was established in 2004, in Sharjah, and we are one of the leading trading companies in the UAE. Our company is a pioneer and the only company in the Middle East that specialises in Hydro- blasting and Friction Testing. We are also the leading organisations in the Supply & Apply of Cold Road Marking Paint, the Supply of Thermoplastic Road Marking Paint (white/yellow) & Glass beads.

Moreover, apart from our Products & Services within the Paint Industry, our company also has a wide range of products in various different industries to offer to its customers, including, Leather Chemicals, Food Chemicals, Electrical & Electronic Equipment’s, IT products and, building materials along with Supplying & Fixing of doors and windows.

We are an ever-growing company who continuously ventures into new industries and markets, and we look forward to fulfilling our customers’ requirements within the given time span and provide a competitive and affordable price.

Projects - here are just a few of our realizations

Reliance Asian Company L.L.C. is a specialist provider of comprehensive solutions across a diverse range of industries. Established in 2004, we have become pioneers in the Middle East for offering advanced Hydro-blasting and Friction Testing services. In addition, we lead the market in the Supply & Application of Cold Road Marking Paint, as well as the Supply of Thermoplastic Road Marking Paint (white/yellow) & Glass Beads for various road projects.

We undertake projects that include end-to-end solutions, encompassing Design and Engineering, Procurement, Project Management, and Application services for road marking and related operations. Our expertise extends to Utility Companies, Construction Firms, and Government Contracts within the road infrastructure sector.

Beyond our core specialization in the paint industry, we also manage a wide range of projects across multiple sectors, including Leather Chemicals, Food Chemicals, Electrical & Electronic Equipment, IT products, and Building Materials. We also offer comprehensive services for Supplying & Fixing doors and windows, ensuring timely execution and quality assurance for all our clients.

As a continuously expanding company, Reliance Asian is committed to entering new markets and industries, delivering efficient, reliable, and affordable solutions tailored to meet the specific needs of each project.
